About me

Hi there, I am a graphic designer based in Spain. I majored in design at Escuela Superior de Diseño de Madrid. Recently mastered in User Experience and User interface design.

Graphic designer & web developer providing creative service and consultancy to international clients in diverse fields – such as culture and politics – keeping close attention to detail and aiming for timeless yet contemporary results.

I mainly create identities for brands, institutions and systems with the aim to elevate their visual language, finding the perfect balance between function/accessibility and aesthetic/culture

In the meantime I explore the potential of data visualization, typeface and illustration :-)
